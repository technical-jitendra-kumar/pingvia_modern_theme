Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and how we can help your business.

What is WhatsApp Business API and how does it benefit me?

WhatsApp Business API is an enterprise-level solution that allows you to automate customer communication, send bulk notifications, and provide support using chatbots. It helps in building trust with a verified green tick profile.

Is there any monthly subscription fee?

Our pricing depends on the plan you choose. While we have setup-based plans, Meta (WhatsApp) charges based on conversations (Utility, Marketing, or Service). We provide a transparent rate card for all services.

How long does it take to get a Verified Green Tick?

The Green Tick verification process usually takes 1-3 business days after your WhatsApp Business API is active. We handle the entire application process for you at no extra cost in our Growth plan.

Can I integrate Pingvia with my existing CRM?

Yes, absolutely! Our APIs are developer-friendly and can be easily integrated with popular CRMs, websites, and third-party applications to automate your workflow.
